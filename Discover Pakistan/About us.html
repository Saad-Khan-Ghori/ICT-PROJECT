Welcome to Discover Pakistan, a collaborative project born out of a shared passion for exploring and showcasing the beauty, diversity, and richness of Pakistan. Our team consists of four dedicated members, each bringing their unique skills and perspectives to the table.


Our journey began as a group project for our ICT course, where we were tasked with creating a website that highlights the cultural, historical, and natural heritage of Pakistan. As we delved deeper into our research, we realized that there was so much more to Pakistan than what meets the eye. From the majestic mountains of the north to the vast deserts of the south, every region has its own distinct flavor, waiting to be discovered.


Our team is comprised of:


  1. Muhammad Wasil, the project lead, who brought the initial idea to life and has been instrumental in guiding the team throughout the project.
  2. Saad Khan Ghori, our lead designer, who has been the driving force behind the design and development of our website, bringing our vision to life.
  3. Hussnain Ayaz and Abdul Wahab have been invaluable team members, providing equal efforts in supporting Wasil and Saad throughout the project. Their moral support, suggestions, and contributions have been essential in helping us stay motivated and focused on our goal.
